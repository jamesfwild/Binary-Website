Learn!

In this section you can learn about how to answer questions covering all of the major topics including converting between denary, binary and hex as well as binary addition and binary shifts! This will allow you to become more comfortable with the thinking behind how to approach a question meaning these questions will be more accessible to you in your exams.

Compete!

Here you can compete with fellow classmates to achieve the top spot in your leaderboards. In this you will be ranked by the amount of questions you have answered correctly, so you will benefit from having a good understanding of these topics as well as frequent use of the website.

Quiz!

In this section you can test your knowledge of what you have covered in the learning area. This can help to reinforce your knowledge and identify your mistakes! This should allow you to become more aware of how exam questions may be presented and how to deal with the time pressures you may be under.
